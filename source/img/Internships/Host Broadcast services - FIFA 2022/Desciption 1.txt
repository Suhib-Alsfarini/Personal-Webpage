HBS is the Host Broadcast Services that films all content related to matches, Live, Interviews etc. then sells it to different distributers like BeinSports and others.

This was the FIFA Legacy Program, designed to integrate students into the professional track of events covering and media with HBS.

I was a Commentary Assistant Intern at Al-Thumama Stadium, and my task was basically make sure all commentary's Microphones boxes are set up correctly before the match, then during the match monitor them from the control room and answer any emergency needed.

Each match wo would have around 80 of those, on peak matches we reach around 100, the skills i learned from this are very diverse but the main one was how to optimize a routine task so you can finish it faster and better each time.
