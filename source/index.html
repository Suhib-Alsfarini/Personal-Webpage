Rihla EV

an App to Promote EV Adoption in Qatar.

This is the first industry led hackathon made by QRDI x Lumi X Iberdrola, it has 5 tracks.

I chose the Problem of Adoption of Electric Vehicles (EVs) in Qatar, the team was very diverse as we had a PhD student specialized in batteries and a business analytics student as well.

We found through our research that the biggest reason is the awareness of the general public and fear of some features.

So for that we came up with an App that tackles the issue of awareness and concerns of the people, with it having some AI features that makes these decisions or features backed by real time data.

The Three main features:

  • Smart trip planner: where you can view all chargers, reviews of it, average waiting and charging time, and the option to pre book the charger
  • Cost Comparison: Compares Between your current petrol car Vs an EV of your choice, Also does that on a break even point analysis and how much you will be saving after it
  • Safety Academy: Provides Basic safety training and knowledge on how to deal with EVs, to tackle the safety concerns

We Also concluded that this needs to be a nation wide movement, so we included plans for collaborations across the country:

  • Charging Stations: we can optimize the scheduling and provide necessary data for the smart trip planner
  • Bus Operators: Deploy a monitoring system for all EVs and provide a call center for any inquiries.

And some other ideas, if you are interested in this feel free to reach out.

Iberdrola Innovation Middle East

Jun - Sep 2024
Iberdrola innovation middle east is an international company with its headquarters being in Bilbao - Spain. The middle east innovation branch focus research focuses on renewable energy and its relevant topics like smart grids, Smart homes, Electric vehicles and mixing AI tools like Computer vision and others for different projects. My internship was in two departments, I started in the power team and my task was to create an AI model to detect a type of fault in the grid that happens when you integrate Type 4 wind turbines as a mean of renewable energy. This fault is named Sub-Synchronous oscillations, and the main effect is that a new frequency will appear in your grid that is below 60 Hz, this naturally is not desired and will damage you system. So i created a simple LSTM Time series based AI model to detect whether the given data has this SSOs or not. Then i moved to the Data science team, where my task was simply to do data annotation, however i was very new to the whole field so i learned all about computer vision and the experience was very useful for my graduation project. And although i never used CV directly, it helped me to communicate clearly what needs to be done and how to view it a project manager.
